C-Mist Card

Size: 85.5mm x 53.2mm

Thickness: 0.7mm

In the front of the C-Mist card, use some layers to show the sky instead of use smooth gradient. In the back of the card, there are three icons, represented for ship, maglev train and double-decker bus which is originate from the UK. These three icons are touchable and interactive, after touch, the screen on the card will show the corresponding route map, if the user want to know more details, they can use the voice assistance that can be arose by touching the button in the top right corner. Also, the screen will show the account detail every time after people consume.

Security design

Fingerprint verification

Before use the card, people need fingerprint verification. The position of it is ergonomic, people can easily complete fingerprint verification while swap the card.

Anti-lost

When the card detected itself falling down in weightlessness and hitting the ground it will brighten and twinkle. This can help people find their card more easily and the card is easier to be seen by people behind the owner. The light will turn off when the card is collected by people. The contact information will be shown in the card in that case.

Bank Card

Size: 85.5mm x 48.1mm

Thickness: 0.7mm

The design style of bank card is similar with the c-mist card. The colorful sky and rich land represent the most precious thing, it is also the yearning for a better life of people now and in the past. In the back, the card number will be encrypted with Morse code. And the color of it is the result of randomly assigned color and background color superimposed. This greatly increases the difficulty of forgery. The screen under it wills show information about consume record and account balance. Also, a greeting sentence to encourage people.

Bank Notes

Size: same size, 155mm x 87mm

The bank notes use 2.5 dimensions style, full of technical sense. In the front of bank notes show two landmarks of Scotland: Carlton Hill and Forth Bridge, in a kind of abstract and three-dimensional way. In the back of 500 debits bank note, it shows a scene of the space, the whole earth is blue and some lights emitted from the surface of the earth, as for its main function, it is for people’s imagination. A planet showing the time can be the moon, in that time, moon has been transformed by human and has more function. In the back of 100 debits bank note, it shows the scene under the sea, cars, roads and any kinds of architecture are built under glass channels.

About coins:

Size:10 creds coin’s diameter is 25mm, 5 creds coin’s diameter is 20mm.
The coins are designed to use materials like stone, shell or meteorite. It is shaped like a piece of chess.The special gloss of the coin is reflected on the white surface. The front shows Carlton Hill and the castle, respectively. The back of the coin, for the value of 10 are sun and king crown, for the value of 5 are moon, stars and queen crown. The crowns stand for the royal of the United Kingdom. The sun and the moon only appear in the sky. These two elements help people to distinguish the coin value from 10 and 5. On the surface of number 5 and 10, there are thin and raised line for blind to touch. There are some complex processed glass on the crown increased difficulty of counterfeiting.
